Our History

From our humble beginnings in a small, refurbished Victorian house, we’ve grown into a beloved local landmark. Our commitment to quality has never wavered, as we’ve meticulously selected each bean and blend to ensure a perfect cup every time. Our baristas, trained in the art of coffee-making, are the heart of our operation, pouring not just coffee but passion into every drink they craft.

As we expanded, so did our menu, evolving from classic brews to innovative concoctions that tantalize the taste buds. Yet, at our core, we’ve remained true to our roots—community and connection. We have hosted countless open mic nights, book clubs, and local charity events, fostering a sense of togetherness that transcends the ordinary.

Join us, and be part of our ever-evolving story.
